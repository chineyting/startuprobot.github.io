Our Services

Business Registration

From J$7,500

If you’re looking to start a business or formalise an existing business, business registration allows you to:

  • Operate legally under a name other than your personal name.
  • Advertise legally.
  • Access business banking and funding.
  • Be exempted from the 3% withholding tax requirement.

Registering a business has different benefits from registering a company. You can convert a business into a company, but you can’t convert a company into a business. Seek legal advice to help you decide which is right for you.

Company Registration

From J$48,000

In order to start a company, you must register it. Registering a company gives you the most of the same benefits as registering a business, but it also allows you to:

  • Raise funds by selling company shares to investors.
  • Protect your personal assets from being seized to pay company debts.

That being said, there are some benefits of registering a business that don’t apply to companies. You can convert a business into a company, but you can’t convert a company into a business. Seek legal advice to help you decide which is right for you.

Tax Compliance Certificate (TCC)

From J$4,500
(plus J$3,500 for each SO2 filed)

A TCC shows that your business/ company is up to date with all of its tax payments. A TCC allows you to:

  • Apply for certain industry licences/registrations.
  • Apply for citizenship or work permits.
  • Apply for government contracts.
  • Apply to clear certain goods through Customs.

TCCs are valid for 3 months, or up to 6 months if your business/company is a PAYE Contributor.

National Contracts Commission Registration (NCC)

From J$6,000

Registering with the National Contracts Commission allows your business/ company to apply for government contracts worth $500,000 or more.

To apply for an NCC, you’ll need:

  1. A TCC.
  2. A Letter of Good Standing from the Companies Office.
  3. A profile of your company.
  4. Qualifications of your project team.
  5. 3 letters of recommendation.

NCCs are valid for 18 months.

Trademarking

From J$26,000

A trademark protects a distinctive visual representation of your brand. Trademarking allows you to:

  • Protect your brand’s reputation by preventing others from using your trademark.
  • Treat your trademark like an asset.
  • Licence the use of your trademark to others.

It can take as long as 10 months to get a trademark, so it’s better to start the process sooner rather than later.
